How Localet Works

Localet allows small businesses to easily track their competition. After a few quick questions, we'll set up a tracker to listen in on any changes big or small from your competitors. We'll then sift through all the changes and send you the most important updates via email.


Step 1: Who are you tracking updates for?

Localet builds your profile by starting with a single company - it can be your own business, or simply one that's emblematic of the businesses you want to track. We'll then put together a short list of companies that compete around that seed business.




Step 2: Who do you want to track?

We'll try our best to come up with a great short list of candidates, but you should have an opportunity to weigh in. Add or remove any other companies you'd like to finish rounding out the tracker. We'll then pervasively monitor the internet for any observable changes involving these companies.




Step 3: What do you want to hear about?

Choose the types of events you want to keep track of. Right now, we break those events down into four groups:

  • Big news like shop closures, major rebrands, and recategorizations of what services the company offers.
  • Profile tweaks like changes in open hours, overall service ratings, price bracket, and senior staff.
  • New reviews across services like Yelp, TripAdvisor, and OpenTable.
  • Rank changes for popular searches in which the companies tracked might show up.
